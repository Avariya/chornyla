## Slimamif typeface (glyph shape derivation)

The single-stroke glyph data in `src/fonts/slimamif.json` was produced by
centerline extraction from the **Slimamif** typeface (style: Slimamif Light)
by **Dmitri Zdorov** (https://dimka.com/fonts).

The original Slimamif font is distributed free for personal and commercial use.
We do not redistribute the original font binary; only derived centerline
coordinate data is included. Attribution is provided here in appreciation of
the original author's work.
